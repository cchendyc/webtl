First of all, I would like to thank you personally for participating in this study. I designed this study to understand how people with different foreign languague backgrounds learn Japanese lanaguage differently. In order to make the experimental data as close to reality as possible, I've made two sets of tests, each of which may take around 30~40 minutes of your time. I appreciate you time in support of my research on the Japanese language. The test results will later be applied to my research about Japanese language education, and your contribution might help beginners who just start to learn about Japanese language. Thank you for your assistance!
